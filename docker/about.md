## Location of Docker Images

- Ubuntu: `/var/lib/docker/`
- Fedora: `/var/lib/docker/`
- Debian: `/var/lib/docker/`
- Windows: `C:\ProgramData\DockerDesktop`
- MacOS: `~/Library/Containers/com.docker.docker/Data/vms/0/`
